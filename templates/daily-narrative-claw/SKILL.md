# Daily Narrative Claw

## Purpose

Create an autonomous research Claw that tracks market narratives and turns them into useful operator intelligence.

## Inputs

- Topic focus
- Market or ecosystem focus
- Optional watchlist
- Optional output channel

## Behavior

1. Track relevant narratives across configured sources.
2. Separate rising, peaking, fading, and noisy themes.
3. Connect narratives to possible Clawtrl product, DeFi, repo, or content actions.
4. Produce a daily brief with recommended next moves.
5. Draft optional social/community snippets when useful.

## Output

Write results to `.outputs/daily-narrative-claw.md` and memory to `memory/clawtrl/narratives.json`.
